Course Management

The purpose of this feature is to make it effortless to find courses, jump back in, and know what’s next. Enrolled courses in grid/list with filters (status, difficulty, tags).Continue where you left off (stores last video timestamp/lesson ID).

Progress tracking Schedules UX notes

Learning Tools

Deepen understanding inside each lesson. keyboard shortcuts, picture-in-picture and autosave notes.average playback speed, quiz correctness first-try vs second-try, note-taking frequency.

Interactive player In-lesson quizzes/assignments Bookmark / Save for later

Practice Hub

Turn knowledge into skills via deliberate practice. Coding challenges with embedded IDE (run tests, show diffs, complexity notes). Aptitude & reasoning bank (topic-tagged, with worked solutions). Timed practice (mock exam mode, section wise timers, review flags).

Coding challenges Timed practice Aptitude & reasoning test

Live Sessions (Subscription)

Real-time learning and mentorship. Live classes with chat, reactions, polls and whiteboard. Real-time Q&A (upvote questions); poll analytics. Recordings auto-indexed by chapters and searchable transcript. Mock interviews (mentor rubric + recorded feedback).

webinars Live classes Mock interviews

Gamification

Sustain motivation and healthy competition. Badges & certificates (verifiable link, shareable). Points & rewards (convert to perks, e.g., 1:1 mentor credits). Leaderboards (friends, cohort, global; opt-in). Streaks (daily/weekly) with gentle recovery grace period.

Personalized challenges Badges & certificates Streaks Points & rewards

Personalization

It makes the dashboard feel “yours”. Custom widgets: drag/drop, resize; pick cards (Progress, To-Do, Goals, Streak, Recommendations, Calendar). Theme: Dark/Light; readable fonts; dyslexia-friendly mode. Learning style presets: video-first / text-first / practice-heavy and adaptive.

Theme Custom widgets Learning style presets

Notifications & Reminders

It's nudge, don’t nag. Tutor announcements and course updates with mute/snooze. In-app, push, email (user-configurable). open/click rates, on-time submission uplift.

Channels Smart digests alerts

Analytics & Reports

It's a Insight → progress. Learning history (timeline, heatmap). Strength/weakness by topic from quizzes & practice. Time spent (daily/weekly) and focus vs passive watch time.

Goals Learning history Time spent

Support & Help

Quick self-serve + real humans when needed. Guides with search and step-by-step articles. Live chat (tutor/support) + ticketing for tech issues. Peer study groups & mentoring (opt-in cohorts, interest tags).

FAQ/Guides Live chat mentoring

Unique Add-ons

Make learning translate to careers and community. Suggests next courses; skill gap analysis; certs to target; portfolio tracker. Challenge friends, private leagues, time-boxed quiz duels with anti-cheat.

Career Roadmap Competitive Learning Mode Data/KPIs
